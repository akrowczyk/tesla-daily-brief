Declining EV Sales: Tesla delivered 1.64M vehicles in 2025, down 9% YoY. Lost global EV crown to BYD (2.26M units). Total revenue fell for the first time in company history. Automotive revenue declined 10% in 2025.

BYD & Chinese Competition: BYD's overseas sales hit 1M vehicles in 2025 (+150% YoY), with plans for 1.6M international sales in 2026. Chinese automakers' European sales surged 95%. BYD's R&D spending exceeds Tesla's.

Execution Risk on AI Pivot: $20B+ CapEx is a massive bet on unproven businesses. Cybercab production using untested "Unboxed" process, Optimus not yet in material factory use, and head of robotics recently departed.
